About DigiBuddy

DigiBuddy is a unique initiative by CollegeTips.in, India’s largest student community, dedicated to making digital literacy accessible and enjoyable for parents and elders. We believe that technology should empower everyone, regardless of age or background. Our mission is to bridge the digital gap and help families stay connected, safe, and confident online.

With a blend of simple video lessons, interactive support, and a friendly approach, DigiBuddy is trusted by thousands of families across India. We’re here to make learning digital skills easy, fun, and stress-free for your loved ones.

Our Story

CollegeTips.in started in 2016 with a vision to help students and their families make the most of the digital world. Over the years, we’ve grown into a vibrant community of over 10 million students, parents, and educators. DigiBuddy was born out of the need to help parents and elders overcome their fear of technology and become confident digital citizens.

  • 10M+ community members
  • 100+ cities across India
  • Thousands of parents empowered
  • Trusted by families, schools, and colleges

What Makes Us Different?
